Before you start

What you'll need

We'll ask for information about:

  • income - how much you (or your partner) get from any jobs, pensions or benefits
  • housing costs - what you (or your partner) pay for things like Council Tax and rent

If you or your partner get them, we may ask you to send us copies of:

  • your payslips
  • your latest Working Tax Credit award notice
  • your latest Universal Credit award notice
Documents that could help

You may need:

  • rent or mortgage statements
  • latest Council Tax bill or Council Tax benefit letter
  • pension details
  • latest statement of benefits or award notice
  • savings, investments and property details

Warning It's important you know this information before you start.
You can't save your application and come back to it later.
