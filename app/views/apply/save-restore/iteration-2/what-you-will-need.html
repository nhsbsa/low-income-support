Before you start

What you'll need

We'll ask for information about:

  • income - how much you get from any pensions or benefits
  • housing costs - what you pay for things like Council Tax and rent

We might ask you to send us copies of your:

  • payslips, if you have a job
  • latest Working Tax Credit award notice, if you get tax credits and have a job
  • Universal Credit award notice, if you get Universal Credit

Documents you'll find helpful

For your own reference, you might need:

  • rent or mortgage statements
  • latest Council Tax bill or Council Tax benefit letter
  • pension details
  • latest statement of benefits or award notice
  • savings, investments and property details
